Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the ChainOS token sale

How do I participate in the token sale?

Connect your Web3 wallet (MetaMask, WalletConnect), complete KYC verification, and purchase $CHOS tokens using ETH, BTC, USDT, USDC, or credit card. Minimum purchase is $100.

When will tokens be distributed?

Token Generation Event (TGE) is scheduled for Q2 2025. 20% of tokens will be unlocked immediately, with the remaining 80% vested linearly over 8 months.

Is ChainOS legal in Estonia?

Yes! We hold a full crypto license from the Estonian Financial Intelligence Unit (FIU). We're fully compliant with EU regulations, AML/KYC requirements, and Estonian crypto laws.

What exchanges will list $CHOS?

We'll launch on DEXs (Uniswap, PancakeSwap) immediately after TGE. Tier 2 CEX listings are confirmed for Q2 2025, with Tier 1 exchanges (Binance, Coinbase) planned for Q3 2025.

Can US citizens participate?

Unfortunately, due to regulatory restrictions, we cannot accept participants from the USA, China, and certain other jurisdictions. Please check our Terms & Conditions for the complete list.

How secure is the smart contract?

Our smart contracts have been audited by CertiK (industry-leading security firm) with a 95/100 security score. The code is open-source and has undergone multiple security reviews. No critical vulnerabilities were found.

Minimum: $100. Maximum: No limit for retail investors. However, large purchases ($100K+) may require additional KYC documentation.
