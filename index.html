What is GoHighLevel (GHL) and why do I need it?

GoHighLevel (GHL) is an all-in-one CRM and marketing automation platform specifically designed for growing businesses. Unlike juggling multiple tools (Mailchimp, Calendly, Zapier), GHL consolidates everything, saving you time and money.

What is n8n workflow automation?

n8n is an open-source workflow automation tool that connects your apps and automates repetitive tasks. Think of it as a more powerful, customizable alternative to Zapier. We use n8n to build custom automations like: syncing data between your CRM and accounting software, automatically creating invoices, sending Slack notifications for new leads, and much more.
